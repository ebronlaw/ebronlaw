About Our Owner

Melody M. Ebron is the founder of Ebron Law Firm LLC. She attended Valdosta State University and obtained her B.A. Degree in Political Science, Cum Laude, in 1999. Then, she continued her education there and earned her M.S. Degree in Criminal Justice in 2001. Mrs. Ebron then attended Thomas M. Cooley Law School where she earned her Juris Doctor (J.D.).


After earning her J.D., Mrs. Ebron became a full-time staff attorney at Georgia Legal Services. While on staff, she handled a wide range of civil legal issues such as divorces, temporary protective orders, housing issues, and consumer issues. However, her area of practice was primarily dedicated to dealing with all legal issues involving the senior community or individuals aged 65 years or older.
